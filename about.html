About Us

Veteran & Family-Owned • 30 Years of Precision

Fundamental Calibrations was founded on a simple principle: deliver exceptional calibration services with honesty, precision, and care. With over 30 years of experience in metrology, we’ve built a reputation for reliability that businesses across Pennsylvania, Delaware, and New Jersey trust.

As a veteran-owned and family-operated business, we bring discipline, attention to detail, and a personal touch to everything we do. Multiple generations work together to ensure every customer receives the highest level of service and respect.

We understand that accurate calibration is critical to your operations. That’s why we focus on fast turnaround, clear communication, and flexible solutions — whether you need standard service, expedited calibration, or on-site support.

Our Mission

"To provide precise, dependable calibration services with the integrity and care our customers deserve."
